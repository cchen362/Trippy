Material discipline Antique foil is confined to hairlines and edge catches. Small labels and confirmation references use flat, contrast-safe color. No metallic body copy, glow, or full-card reflection.

Recommended direction · 03

Obsidian ink with concierge detailing

It feels authored for Trippy rather than borrowed from a luxury checkout flow. The inset keyline, warmer mineral surface, and restrained paper-edge cues distinguish the family while leaving dense data calm.

Use satin as the quiet fallback for dense secondary panels. Reserve lacquer for one hero-level booking state, if it is used at all. The booking family itself should adopt obsidian.
