Kuanzhai Alley Historic Blocks (宽窄巷子历史文化街区)

Three restored Qing-dynasty lanes — Wide, Narrow, and Well — where courtyard teahouses, ear-cleaning stalls, and Sichuan snack vendors share stone walls with boutique hotels. The area rewards a slow, unplanned wander more than a checklist.

Fits your slow-mornings pace — best before 10:00.

Local insight

Wide Alley is for photographs, Narrow Alley is for eating, and Well Alley is where the neighbourhood actually sits — take tea at the courtyard furthest from the gate.
